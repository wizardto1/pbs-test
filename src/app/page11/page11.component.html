#11: Step Over Obstacles (20 ft / 6 m)

Tester: Measure and mark a 20 ft (6 m) walkway between 2 tapes. Place 2 stacked shoe boxes 10 ft away from the start. Guard client. Look for hesitation, short steps and touching the boxes during task. Demonstrate the task. Allow 1 practice trial. Indicate assistive device, if used.

Client: Stand, begin walking at your usual speed. When you get to the boxes, step over them, not around them, and keep walking. Try not to touch the boxes.
