#20: Gait with Eyes Closed (20 ft /6 m)

Tester: Measure and mark a 20 ft (6 m) walkway between 2 tapes. Ask the client to walk with eyes closed. Guard client. Watch for eyes opening and deviating from center of walkway. Demonstrate the task. Allow 1 practice trial. Time task. Indicate assistive device, if used.

Client: Stand, toes against the tape. I will ask you to walk forward at your usual speed from here to the farthest tape with your eyes closed. Close your eyes and walk as straight as you can until I say stop. I will start timing when you close your eyes and begin walking.

≤ 7 s with no imbalance AND ≤ 6 in (15 cm) max deviation from center of walkway
